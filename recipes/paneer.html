Panner Butter Masala

Description

Paneer Butter Masala is a popular Indian dish, where paneer (cottage cheese) is cooked in a rich, creamy tomato-based gravy, often flavored with butter, spices, and cashew nuts.

It's known for its smooth texture and mildly spicy taste.

Enjoy it with naan or rice for a satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer (Indian cottage cheese)
  • 2 medium-sized onions, finely chopped
  • 2 medium-sized tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • 1 tsp garlic paste
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala powder
  • 2 tbsp cashew nuts (optional, for creaminess)
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp fresh cream (for garnish)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cut the paneer into cubes and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat 1 tbsp of butter and oil. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle.
  3. Add chopped onions and saut√© until they turn golden brown.
  4. Add ginger and garlic paste, cook for a minute.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes, coriander powder, cumin powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes are soft and mushy.
  6. Blend the mixture into a smooth paste. You can add some water if needed.
  7. Transfer the paste back to the pan, add water and bring it to a boil.
  8. Add cashew nuts (if using) and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  9. Add paneer cubes and garam masala powder. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  10. Stir in the remaining butter and fresh cream.
  11. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve with naan or rice.
